  • December 12, 2011. A review of my PhD dissertation, written by Shlomo Argamon (Illinois Institute of Technology) was published in Literary and Linguistic Computing. You can read it here. Conclusion (quoted from the review by S. Argamon): The way forward in authorship attribution research is, primarily, clarification and standardization of experimental methodology and the concomitant development of a body of rigorous (and general) empirical results. 'Scalable Issues' is a significant contribution to the field which helps to move it in this direction. The book is well worth reading, both for the specialist in authorship studies as well as the technically savvy non-specialist with an interest in the field.
